About CineArtist

CineArtist is a passion-driven film space by Himanshu Bharti – focused on storytelling, emotions and relatable characters.

From writing and direction to editing and production, every project is crafted with a cinematic approach, keeping real life and real people at the center.

Short films, sketches, social messages and experimental visuals – CineArtist is a playground to explore ideas and connect with the audience.

This website is a home for all CineArtist projects – films, stories, casting and collaborations.
