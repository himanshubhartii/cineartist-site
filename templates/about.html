About CineArtist

A small-town YouTube experiment that grew into a space for cinematic short films and emotional storytelling.

Our Journey

CineArtist originally started as SANM Captures — a small creative attempt made in a small town with one simple aim: to tell stories that felt honest, relatable and full of emotion.

With no professional camera or big setup, everything began with a basic phone camera, homemade lighting and a passionate team experimenting with short films after school, college and work. Slowly, people started connecting with the raw, real-life inspired stories.

In 2024, SANM Captures evolved into CineArtist — a name that represents cinematic storytelling, simple visuals and expressive characters.

Today, CineArtist continues to create short films, stories and character moments with the same belief: "Real emotions and simple visuals can touch people deeply."

From Then to Now

  • Past: Started as SANM Captures with a phone camera and homemade lights.
  • Place: Born in a small-town environment full of real stories and real people.
  • 2024: Rebranded to CineArtist to expand creative vision.
  • Now: A YouTube space for cinematic short films & emotional storytelling.
