Casting & Auditions

Share your details if you want to act in upcoming CineArtist short films and concepts.

How casting works

We look for natural performances, expressions and honesty in front of the camera. No big portfolio is required – just genuine interest and commitment.

  • Short films, sketches and character-based stories
  • Mostly shoot in and around local locations
  • Both new and experienced actors are welcome

Helpful to share

  • Any previous acting or stage experience (if you have)
  • Link to Instagram / YouTube / Reel / Portfolio
  • City & availability for shoot
